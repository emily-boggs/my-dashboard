# Product Context

### Why This Dashboard Exists

FastForward Logistics operates across multiple regions and manages a high volume of shipments
at any given time. The Operations Team is responsible for monitoring the health of those
shipments daily — but today, that means jumping between multiple tools, pulling manual reports,
and piecing together a picture of performance on their own.

This dashboard exists to change that. It gives the Ops Team one place to see everything that
matters — shipment volume, delivery performance, regional trends, and anything that needs
attention — without the friction of manual data gathering.

---

### Who Uses It

| User | Role |
|---|---|
| **Operations Team** | Primary users. Monitor daily shipment health, track performance metrics, and respond to exceptions. |

This is a **shared internal dashboard** — there are no individual logins or personalized views.
Anyone on the Ops Team can open it and get the same real-time picture of the business.

---

### Problems It Solves

- **Fragmented visibility** — The Ops Team currently has no single source of truth for
logistics performance. This dashboard consolidates the most important metrics into one view.

- **Slow exception response** — Delayed shipments, failed deliveries, and customs holds can
go unnoticed when data lives in multiple places. The exceptions panel surfaces these
immediately so the team can act faster.

- **Manual reporting overhead** — Time spent pulling and compiling reports is time not spent
solving problems. This dashboard eliminates that overhead for day-to-day monitoring.

- **Lack of regional insight** — Performance can vary significantly by region. The dashboard
makes it easy to compare regions and drill into areas that need attention.

---

### UX Goals

- **Clarity first** — The dashboard should communicate the most important information
immediately, without requiring the user to dig or interpret.

- **Low cognitive load** — Clean layout, consistent visual language, and clear labeling so
users can scan quickly and act confidently.

- **Feels fast** — Smooth animations and a responsive layout make the experience feel
polished and professional, not sluggish or clunky.

- **Dark mode by default** — Designed for people who may be staring at this screen for long
periods. Dark mode reduces eye strain and sets a focused, professional tone.

- **Accessible at a glance** — KPIs, map, and exceptions should all be visible without
scrolling on a standard desktop screen where possible.
